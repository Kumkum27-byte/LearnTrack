About GrowLog

GrowLog is a practical learning tracker for students and self-learners who want consistency—not just notes. You can log daily study sessions, measure streaks, monitor progress with charts, and plan upcoming study tasks with a built-in scheduler.

From first learning day to long-term habits, GrowLog keeps your progress visible and organized with history filters, goals, and a dashboard that turns your effort into insights.

✓ Built for DSA, Web Dev, ML, and any learning path
✓ Includes logs, streaks, analytics, goals, and scheduling
✓ Free, open source, and easy to extend
